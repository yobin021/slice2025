Description

Welcome to Paper Presentation, the ultimate platform to showcase your innovative research and project ideas. Registration:

•Registration will be online only – No on-spot registrations.

•Registration closes on 21st August 2025 for the symposium scheduled on 23rd August 2025.

•Teams must upload their PPT in the registration form itself.

•Plagiarism: Original work only — copied content will lead to disqualification
