Description

Byte The Bomb is a thrilling 2-round tech challenge that blends hardware skills, logical reasoning, and high-pressure problem-solving into one explosive experience. Designed for teams of two, this event welcomes participants from all departments — no deep electronics knowledge required!

Will your team defuse the bomb (dummy) before time runs out? Or will the wires get the best of you?

Flow of Event

Round 1: Build the RNG (Random Number Generator)

Time: 45 minutes

Teams will receive all the components and documents needed to build a basic RNG using:

💣 IC 555 Timer

💣 IC 4026

💣 Digital Trainer Kit

💣 Other essential components

Goal:

Successfully create a single-digit RNG (0–9). This will act as your “digital dice” for the next round.

Functionality, and time of accomplishment will be scored.


Round 2: The Board Game – “Circuit Chase”

15 minutes

Each team will receive:

💣 A tech-themed board game sheet.

💣 A bomb (dummy) with 4 coloured wires.

💣 Their own RNG circuit from Round 1.

Goal:

Advance across the board using your RNG. Along the way:

💣 Solve logic and tech puzzles on trap tiles.

💣 Land on clue tiles that reveal incorrect wire colours.

💣 Reach the end of the board before time runs out.

💣 Use the 3 clues to eliminate the wrong wires.

• Cut the correct wire to defuse the bomb!

✘ Choose wrong? You blow the mission.

✔Choose right? Bomb defused, glory earned.
