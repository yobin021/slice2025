Description

Team of 3 to 4 participants assigned for each table with a laptop. Each laptop contains 3 set of questions. A set contains text file and cmd files. In cmd window qn will be displayed. After clearing the 3 questions and an escape phrase will be displayed in cmd window to complete the event. Winners are chosen based on the timing.

Flow of Event

🌐The team will enter the room.

🌐The rules and regulations will be explained by the instructor.

🌐A stipulated time of 7 minutes will be provided to solve the puzzle.

🌐The team will be provided with a laptop where they have to decode the puzzle in order to find the out-phrase, to escape the room.

🌐The team that fails to tell the out-phrase within the time given, will have to leave the room.

🌐NOTE : The laptop provided to the participants will be completely formatted and doesn’t have network access and the instructor will be monitoring the participants throughout the event.
