Description

An exclusive platform for aspiring innovators to pitch their prototypes or ideas, tackle real-world problems, and showcase entrepreneurial thinking.

From innovation to business strategy – prove you have what it takes to be the next game-changer.


✨ Special Opportunity:

The Top 2 teams will not only be recognized by a real entrepreneur but will also get a chance to present their idea at the LICET Incubation Cell a platform to take your project closer to a real startup journey.


Don't miss this opportunity

Flow of Event

Round 1: Pitch the Innovation

🚀Each team gets 8 minutes (6 minutes pitch + 2 minutes Q&A).

🚀Teams present their idea/prototype and problem statement.

🚀Teams can have 1–3 members.

🚀Judges evaluate on innovation, feasibility, clarity, and problem relevance.

🚀Top 5 teams will be shortlisted for Round 2.


Round 2: Business Battle

Shortlisted teams present the business side of their innovation.

Must cover:

🚀 Marketing Strategy

🚀Revenue Model

🚀Funding Process

🚀Implementation Roadmap

• Judges will evaluate entrepreneurial and market-oriented thinking.
